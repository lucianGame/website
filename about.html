About me

Hello! My name is Lucian Meisler. I am a multimedia artist practicing music, sound design, and game development. I am based in Los Angeles and am currently doing freelance work as a technical sound designer. The software I work with includes: Unity, Unreal, FMOD, Wwise, Pro Tools, Logic Pro, and Blender.
