Games

Ant World

A rowdy snake has broken into the ant tunnel, and you're the only one who can stop him! Ant World is an ongoing project created in Unity.

Witch Hunt

Play as a young witch looking to avenge her sister in this first person shooter. Witch Hunt was made for Jame Gam #40, a five day game jam, using Unreal Engine.

Take in the scenery and relax a little! Walk in the Park is a walking simulator created in Unity as an FMOD integration practice project.

Ant World: the VR Experience

Explore the ant tunnels from a first person point of view in a virtual reality world! Ant World: the VR Experience was created in Unity for the Meta Quest 2.
